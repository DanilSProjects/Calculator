Calculator

A most revolutionary tool. Now you no longer have to do those troublesome sums in your head, but instead have a machine do it for you - truly the future!*

Special keyboard shortcuts: ESC to clear, BACKSPACE to delete, ENTER/= to get answer.

* It can only do one operation at a time, though. So BODMAS/PEMDAS/MVEMJSUN will not do you any good here.
